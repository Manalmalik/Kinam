Social Projects


With KINAM’s social projects, we aim to build pride and appreciation for the Maya culture amongst younger generations and equip them with the necessary tools for future success. Our three first projects are: education and skill-building through football, re-introduction of Maya art by teaching descendants how to read and write in original Glyphs, and financing Maya archeological projects to uncover lost treasures.
Another long term goal in the pipeline is to create volunteer participation projects for people from all around the world whereby we can stimulate an even greater cultural exchange. There is an oppurtunity for social projects in Guatemaia to have a true impact,to preserve and protect the rich ancient Maya culture.
