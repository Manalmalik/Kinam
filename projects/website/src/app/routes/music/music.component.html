Music

From the beginning, KINAM’s mission was to share meaningful stories with people to form new emotional connections across the globe. That is why the co-founders of KINAM decided to combine design, fashion, music and video production to form a new integrated social impact brand to tell stories in all forms of tangible and intangible media. The journey took the team to Guatemala, to record live sounds from the jungle to tell the legends of the ancient Maya civilization and shoot the crowdfunding video for the entire project.
They aim to completely immerse the listener into the Maya world with music based on actual legends described in the Mayan’s sacred scripture, the “popol vuh”, artworks portraying the stories’ iconic scenery and a comprehensible version of the story to read on their website. As for all KINAM incomes, 10% of all streaming revenues will go back to Guatemala to support the social projects of KINAM.
